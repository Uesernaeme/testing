# Guacamole
## Ingredients
* avocado: 1€
* lime: 1€
* salt: 0.01€
## Instructions
1. cut avocado in half
2. remove avocado pit
3. scoop up avocado pulp into a bowl
4. cut lime in half
5. squeeze lime into bowl
6. drop a pinch of salt into bowl
4. mix and mush everything to obtain a creamy consistency
